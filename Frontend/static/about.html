About

Welcome to Canine Impact AI, where advanced technology meets dental expertise. We are a pioneering dental AI platform dedicated to providing accurate and reliable analyses of panoramic X-rays to assess the severity of canine impaction.

Our Mission

Our mission is to leverage the power of artificial intelligence to assist dental professionals in diagnosing and planning treatment for impacted canines. We aim to improve patient outcomes by offering an innovative tool that delivers quick and precise analysis, helping clinicians make informed decisions.

What is Canine Impaction?

Canine impaction is a common dental condition where the canine teeth fail to erupt properly, becoming stuck in the jawbone or gum tissue. This condition can lead to complications such as misaligned teeth, gum disease, or damage to surrounding structures. Early detection and proper treatment are key to preventing further issues and ensuring a healthy smile.
